PANEER MAKHANI







Ingredients:


-200g paneer, cut into cubes
-2 tbsp oil
-1 tsp cumin seeds
-2-3 green cardamom pods
-1 onion, finely chopped
-1 tsp ginger paste
-1 tsp garlic paste
-2-3 tomatoes, finely chopped
-1 tsp red chilli powder
-1 tsp coriander powder
-1/2 tsp garam masala powder
-Salt to taste
-1/4 cup cream
-1 tbsp butter
-Coriander leaves for garnishing

	

Instructions:

Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds and cardamom pods. Sauté for a minute.
Add the onion and sauté until it turns translucent.
Add the ginger paste and garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
Add the tomatoes, red chilli powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, and salt to taste. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
Remove from heat and let it cool. Blend to a smooth paste using a blender or food processor.
Heat butter in a pan and add the blended mixture. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
Add the paneer cubes and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Add the cream and cook for another minute.
Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan. Enjoy your Paneer Makhani

	
Note: The recipe can be adjusted according to personal preferences, such as adding 
    	more or less spices or adjusting the cooking time.
