VEG BIRYANI







Ingredients:


1 cup basmati rice
2 cups water
1 tbsp oil
1 bay leaf
2-3 green cardamoms
2-3 cloves
1 inch cinnamon stick
1 onion, thinly sliced
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tomato, finely chopped
1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, etc.), chopped
1/4 cup yogurt
1 tsp red chili powder
1 tsp garam masala powder
Salt to taste
2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves, finely chopped

      

	

Instructions:

Soak rice in water for 30 minutes.
Heat oil in a pan and add bay leaf, cardamoms, cloves, and cinnamon. Let them splutter.
Add onion and sauté until it turns golden brown.
Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
Add tomato and cook until it turns soft and mushy.
Add mixed vegetables and cook for 3-4 minutes.
Add yogurt, red chili powder, garam masala powder, and salt. Mix well.
Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pan. Mix well.
Add 2 cups of water and bring it to a boil.
Cover and cook on low flame until the rice is cooked and all the water is absorbed.
Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with raita or curry.

	
Note: The recipe can be adjusted according to personal preferences, such as adding 
    	more or less spices or adjusting the cooking time.
