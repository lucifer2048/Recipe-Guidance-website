CHICKEN HARIYALI




    Chicken Hariyali is a delicious Indian dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a creamy, 
    flavorful sauce with fresh green herbs. 
    Here's a recipe to make Chicken Hariyali:





Ingredients:

- 500g boneless chicken, cut into small pieces
- 1 cup yogurt
- 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
- 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
- 2 green chilies, chopped
- 1 tbsp ginger paste
- 1 tbsp garlic paste
- 1 tsp cumin powder
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 1 tsp garam masala powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt to taste
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 1 tomato, chopped
- 1/2 cup cream
- Coriander leaves for garnishing

    

	

Instructions:

1. In a blender, add yogurt, coriander leaves, mint leaves, green chilies, ginger paste, 
garlic paste, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, turmeric powder, 
lemon juice, and salt. Blend until smooth.
2. In a bowl, add the chicken pieces and the blended mixture. 
Mix well and marinate for at least 30 minutes or up to 4 hours in the refrigerator.
3. Heat oil in a pan and add chopped onions. Saute until the onions turn golden brown.
4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
5. Add the marinated chicken along with the marinade. Cook for 10-15 minutes on medium heat or 
until the chicken is cooked through.
6. Add cream and mix well. Simmer for 5-10 minutes on low heat.
7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan bread. 
Enjoy your Chicken Hariyali!

	
Note: The recipe can be adjusted according to personal preferences, such as adding 
    	more or less spices or adjusting the cooking time.
