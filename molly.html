FISH MOLLY




    Fish Molly is a traditional Kerala dish made with fish, coconut milk, and spices. 
    Here's a recipe to make Fish Molly:



Ingredients:

	• 500g fish fillets (any white fish)
    •1 cup coconut milk
    •1 onion, thinly sliced
    •1 tomato, chopped
    •1-inch ginger, minced
    •3 garlic cloves, minced
    •2 green chilies, slit
    •1 tsp red chili powder
    •1/2 tsp turmeric powder
    •1 tsp coriander powder
    •1/2 tsp garam masala powder
    •1 tsp mustard seeds
    •1 sprig curry leaves
    •2 tbsp oil
    •Salt to taste
    

	

Instructions:

Clean and cut the fish into medium-sized pieces. 
Marinate the fish with salt, turmeric, and red chili powder for 30 minutes.
Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. 
Once the mustard seeds crackle, add curry leaves, sliced onions, minced ginger, minced garlic, 
and slit green chilies. Saute until the onions turn golden brown.
Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
Add coriander powder, garam masala powder, and salt. 
Saute for a minute.
Add the marinated fish pieces and cook for 5 minutes on medium heat.
Add coconut milk and bring it to a boil. 
Reduce the heat and cook

	
Note: The recipe can be adjusted according to personal preferences, such as adding
more or less spices or adjusting the cooking time.
