CHICKEN 65




    Chicken 65 is a popular South Indian dish that is spicy, crispy, and delicious. 
	It is usually served as an appetizer or a snack. 
	Here's a recipe to make Chicken 65:




Ingredients:

- 500g boneless chicken, cut into small pieces
- 1/2 cup yogurt
- 1 tsp ginger paste
- 1 tsp garlic paste
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tsp garam masala powder
- 1 tsp cumin powder
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 1 tsp black pepper powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup cornflour
- 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
- Oil for deep frying
- Curry leaves for garnishing
- Lemon wedges for serving
    

	

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, mix the yogurt, ginger paste, garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, 
garam masala powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, black pepper powder, and salt. 
Add the chicken pieces and mix well. Marinate for at least 30 minutes or up to 
4 hours in the refrigerator.
2. In another bowl, mix the cornflour and all-purpose flour.
3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan.
4. Take each chicken piece and coat it in the flour mixture. Shake off the excess flour 
and drop the chicken piece into the hot oil. Fry until the chicken is golden brown and crispy. 
Repeat with the remaining chicken pieces.
5. Garnish with curry leaves and serve hot with lemon wedges. 
Enjoy your Chicken 65!

	
Note: The recipe can be adjusted according to personal preferences, such as adding 
    	more or less spices or adjusting the cooking time.
