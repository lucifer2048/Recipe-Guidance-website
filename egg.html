EGG MANCHURIAN




    Egg Manchurian is a delicious Indo-Chinese dish that combines deep-fried egg balls with a 
    sweet and tangy sauce. 
    Here's a recipe to make Egg Manchurian:






Ingredients:

For the Egg Balls:

- 4 eggs, beaten
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/4 cup cornstarch
- 1 tsp ginger paste
- 1 tsp garlic paste
- Salt to taste
- Oil for deep frying


For the Sauce:

- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 1 green pepper, chopped
- 1 tbsp ginger paste
- 1 tbsp garlic paste
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- Salt to taste


    

	

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, mix together beaten eggs, all-purpose flour, cornstarch, ginger paste, garlic paste, 
and salt. Mix well to make a thick batter.
2. Heat oil in a deep frying pan.
3. Take spoonfuls of the batter and drop them into the hot oil. 
Fry until the egg balls turn golden brown and crispy. 
Drain them on a paper towel to remove excess oil.
4. In another pan, heat 2 tbsp oil and add chopped onions and green peppers. 
Saute until they turn slightly soft.
5. Add ginger paste and garlic paste. Saute for a minute.
6. Add soy sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, brown sugar, and salt. Mix well and saute for a minute.
7. In a small bowl, mix cornstarch with 1/2 cup of water. Add this mixture to the pan and stir well.
8. Bring the mixture to a boil and let it simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.
9. Add the fried egg balls to the sauce and mix well.
10. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy your Egg Manchurian!

	
Note: The recipe can be adjusted according to personal preferences, such as adding 
    	more or less spices or adjusting the cooking time.
